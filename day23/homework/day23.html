About the Movie

Rocky Balboa (2006) is the sixth installment in the "Rocky" series, starring Sylvester Stallone as the iconic boxer Rocky Balboa. In this film, the aging boxer comes out of retirement for one last fight against the reigning heavyweight champion, Mason Dixon.

Cast

  • Sylvester Stallone as Rocky Balboa
  • Michael B. Jordan as Apollo Creed's son
  • Talia Shire as Adrian Pennino
